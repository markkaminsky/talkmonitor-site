Common questions

How do I start a session?

Open TalkMonitor, go to the Now tab, and tap Start. The app listens through your iPhone microphone and sends haptic nudges when your voice goes above the level you've set. If you'd rather not tap at all, turn on Settings → While monitoring → Start listening when the app opens — opening the app then starts the session for you. Listening only ever happens while the app is open.

Why am I not getting haptic nudges?

Check these first:

  1. Make sure a session is running (tap Start on the Now tab).
  2. Confirm microphone permission is granted: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → TalkMonitor → on.
  3. Check that your volume threshold in Settings isn't set so high that normal speech never triggers it — the app tells you on the Now screen if your level never comes near your line.
  4. Make sure haptics are on in the app (Settings → Voice & feel → Haptics (silent feedback)), and try raising the Voice intensity slider in Settings → Comfort & awareness if taps feel too faint.
  5. Confirm system haptics are enabled: iOS Settings → Sounds & Haptics → System Haptics on.

What is a "heated moment"?

When your level and pace climb together while the tone of recent words trends tense — all read on your device — TalkMonitor softens early: a small card appears with a grounding line, a few soft things you could say, and a graceful way to pause, and if Settle mode is on, a slow breath-paced haptic pulse starts too. It's a gentle read of the moment, not argument detection. Turn it on or off in Settings → Comfort & awareness → Notice heated moments.

What are "Soft lines"?

The same advice card, on demand. During any session, tap the Soft lines pill under the orb — useful when you're walking into a conversation you already know is tense. The wording is a curated library grounded in published de-escalation research; if you turn on Suggest soft responses (shown once Notice heated moments is on), on-device Apple Intelligence tailors the lines to what's being discussed.

Why don't I hear the spoken lines?

By design, spoken advice plays only through a connected personal listening route — headphones, AirPods, or other Bluetooth audio — and never the phone's built-in speaker. If you're paired to a shared Bluetooth speaker or a car stereo, disconnect it first: the app can't tell one Bluetooth output from another, so anything paired over Bluetooth will carry the lines. If you hear nothing: check that Speak soft lines in headphones is on in Settings → Comfort & awareness (it appears once Notice heated moments is on), and that headphones are actually connected. A soft two-note tone plays just before the words so you don't miss the first line; you can turn the tone off separately.

What are intention cards?

Before a session, the Now screen offers five small cards — like Understand first or Start gently. Pick one and the advice card leads with that intention's wording when a moment heats up. Tap it again to clear it.

Why isn't my speaking pace (WPM) showing?

Pace uses on-device speech recognition. Grant speech recognition when prompted, or enable it in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition → TalkMonitor.

If your device or language doesn't support on-device recognition, TalkMonitor may use Apple's server-side speech APIs to produce the transcript. All transcript features — pace, word matching, and the tone trend — then run from that result on your device, the same way. The app shows a note on the Now screen when that happens.

What are session reflections?

After you stop a session, TalkMonitor saves a summary to Recent. On supported devices, optional on-device Apple Intelligence can write a short reflection from session stats (duration, average volume, pace, alerts). No raw audio or transcript is sent to us or stored in the cloud.

You can turn rich reflections on or off in Settings.

Does TalkMonitor record or upload my conversations?

No. Audio is processed on your iPhone in real time. We don't run servers, don't store your voice, and don't share your data. Session history (stats and optional reflection text) stays on your device unless you delete it.

See our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Session history is stored locally on your iPhone. To remove it:

  • Delete individual sessions from the Recent tab, or
  • Delete the TalkMonitor app — all local session data is removed with the app.

TalkMonitor has no account. There is nothing for us to delete on a server.

Is TalkMonitor a medical or therapy app?

No. TalkMonitor is a general wellness and self-awareness companion. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any medical condition.
